About me

Hi there, I'm Aron Bencsik - an Artificial Intelligence enthusiast.

Currently, I'm pursuing a Master's degree in Advanced Computer Science with AI at The University of Manchester.

I graduated with a first-class Bachelor's degree in Computer Science with AI from Northumbria University. My thesis centred around computational neuroscience, with the aim to detect fraudelent stock market data.

As a Machine Learning Intern at the Institute for Computer Science and Control in Budapest, I acquired valuable insight into applying machine learning to the classification of digital holographic microscope images.

During my internship as an AI developer at Northumbria University, I investigated the optimization of human-machine cooperation using multi-agent reinforcement learning.
